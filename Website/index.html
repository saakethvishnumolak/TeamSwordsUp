StatTrackr

What is StatTrackr

Unlike Huddle, or our other competitors, StatTrackr is specifically designed for college teams, and provides unique access to other school’s stats and data. Through StatTrackr, coaches can compare their team’s performance to the competitions, ensuring that your athletes are always ready for game day!

Who?

For coaches and schools within the NCAA who need an efficient system to keep track of performance-related statistics

"Track more, See more, Win more"

Our vision

Sporting performance is becoming increasingly about data and stats which is becoming a fundamental component of any sports team. This tracker would be used to boost performance from the data they receive to tailor training and focus on their strengths and weaknesses. This is used in many sporting teams around the globe and there is a need for this market in collegiate sports teams.

Features

  • Keep Track of you Data - StatTrackr helps you keep track of your team's performance data
  • Share Conference Data - StatTrackr shares other teams stats, giving you access to thousands
  • Create Game Predicitions - StatTrackr can compare the information between two schools, and givean unbiased predicition based on registered data
