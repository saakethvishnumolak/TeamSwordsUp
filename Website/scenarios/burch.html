Scenario One - Comeback

During a key game, Alex is directing his son's basketball team. The squad is down at the break, and Alex needs to rethink his strategy. He wishes he had a sports tracking app that would enable him to instantly access comprehensive player performance information, shooting percentages, and the strengths and weaknesses of the opposition, assisting him in fine-tuning the team's strategy for a comeback in the second half.


User Stories

  • As a basketball coach, I want to access live player performance statistics during the game so that I can make data-informed decisions for strategy adjustments at halftime.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to view shooting percentages for my team's players in real-time so that I can identify who is having a hot hand and should get more opportunities to score.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to analyze the opponent's strengths and weaknesses on the fly so that I can exploit their vulnerabilities and create a winning strategy for the second half.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to receive in-game notifications about key player milestones, such as scoring records or personal bests, to motivate and celebrate player achievements.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to access historical performance data for my team, so I can compare current performance to past games and identify trends that may affect our strategy.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to quickly share relevant statistics with my assistant coaches and team members so that we can collectively make informed decisions to improve our performance.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to create custom dashboards to focus on specific performance metrics, making it easier to track what matters most for our team's success.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to ensure that the sports tracking app's interface is user-friendly and intuitive, as I need to access critical data rapidly during the game without any technical hiccups.

Scenario Two - Tryouts!

On a weekend, Alex is working with his kid and the younger members of the team in a tryouts period. He wants to monitor each player through the week of tryouts. He is using an id camp to help scout players and wants a sports tracking app to get real game statistics in order to analyze each player that he may be recruiting. Alex feels this app would get him more vital information in the recruitment process which can be crucial to narrowing down each player if they are struggling to decide who to get.

Once the coaching staff have a meeting to themselves analyzing this data in a spreadsheet etc., they feel they have important data to reject players. It is a lot easier to sit a player down and tell them where to improve for whichever team they decide to try out for next.


User Stories

  • As a basketball coach, I want to track individual player development progress over time during player development sessions, so I can tailor my coaching to address each player's specific needs and strengths.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to record and monitor key player performance metrics such as shooting accuracy, dribbling skills, and speed during player development sessions, helping me provide personalized coaching feedback.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to use the sports tracking app to collect real game statistics for players during an ID camp, enabling me to evaluate potential recruits more effectively.
  • As a basketball coach, I want the app to provide detailed player statistics, including points scored, rebounds, assists, and defensive plays, to assess a player's overall performance during recruitment events.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to be able to add notes and observations for each player within the app, allowing me to highlight areas of improvement or exceptional skills when considering them for recruitment.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to analyze player data within the app and create player profiles for potential recruits, streamlining the player selection process and facilitating better decision-making during recruitment.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to be able to share player performance statistics and profiles with the coaching staff so that we can collectively assess and discuss which players to recruit.
  • As a basketball coach, I want the sports tracking app to export player data to a spreadsheet or other analysis tools for more in-depth analysis and discussion during coaching staff meetings.

Scenario Three - Post Game Pep Talk

After a game the previous weekend, when Alex is gathering his team for some post-match analysis, he wants to do a player specific session. He wants to monitor each person’s individual performance away from a team environment. He would use this information for post-game analysis. In order to adapt the coaching to help look at both the strengths and weaknesses of each player, a sport’s tracking app would be great for capturing performance data, from the likes of shooting ability to defensive blocks.


User Stories

  • As a basketball coach, I want to have access to individual player performance data after the game, so I can conduct personalized post-match analysis sessions to help players understand their strengths and weaknesses.
  • As a basketball coach, I want the sports tracking app to record and analyze each player's shooting accuracy, three-point shooting percentage, and free-throw performance, allowing me to provide tailored shooting improvement strategies.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to track a player's defensive skills, including blocks, steals, and defensive rebounds, using the app to create targeted defensive training plans for each player.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to measure a player's speed and agility by using the sports tracking app, helping me design customized conditioning and agility drills.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to gather statistics on each player's ball-handling and passing skills, enabling me to offer specific training and drills to improve their playmaking abilities.
  • As a basketball coach, I want the app to provide a clear visualization of each player's performance metrics, making it easier to identify trends, strengths, and areas for improvement in post-match analysis sessions.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to be able to compare a player's current game performance with their historical data to track their progress over time, helping to set realistic development goals.
  • As a basketball coach, I want to share individual player performance reports with each player privately, highlighting their accomplishments and areas for growth, promoting player development and motivation.
