Andrew Martin

Scenario One - Learning From the Pros

Andrew is a forward for the University of Alabama men's basketball team. The team has played in their first few games of the season, and at this point, each player has a general idea of how they rank among their teammates. Andrew has noticed that, while he’s been one of the best layup shooters on the team, he struggles a lot when shooting free throws. Compared to his peers on StatTrackr, he currently ranks 10th out of 14 when looking at the team’s free throw percentage.

His teammate Kyle, meanwhile, has been one of the strongest close-shooters on the team. He’s currently the third-best all-around shooter on the team, and he’s among the top 200 close-shooters on StatTrackr. Andrew noticed this, and asked Kyle one day after practice if he could help him with his free throws.

Kyle was glad to help, and gave Andrew some advice on how he can improve his aim. Afterwards, Andrew found himself shooting better thanks to Kyle’s advice, and became much better at close shooting. Over the next few weeks, Andrew’s average free throw percentage went up, and he ended the season ranking 5th out of 14 on StatTrackr.


User Stories

  • As an athlete, I want to be able to compare my statistics against my teammates because it will give me an idea of how well I am doing compared to the rest of my team.
  • As an athlete, I want to see numerically how I rank against my teammates because it will give me a faster and clearer understanding of how my performance compares against my peers.
  • As an athlete, I want to see numerically how I rank against my teammates because it can alert me to potential shortcomings within my performance.
  • As an athlete, I want to see if my statistics are ranked highly among other athletes because I will be more motivated to keep practicing and playing if I can see that I’m doing well.
  • As an athlete, I need to have the ability to regularly incorporate new data into my statistics because it will help me quickly see how my statistics have improved or worsened throughout the season, keeping me up to my performance trends.
  • As an athlete, I need to be able to compare my past averages against my current ones because it will help me easily see how each new game has affected my overall stats, allowing me to see key points throughout the year where my skills really improved.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to see who the best players on my team are because I want to know which teammate would be able to help me the most whenever I’m facing a skill issue.
  • As an athlete, I need to be able to compare specific skills against my teammates because it can let me know what each of our strengths and weaknesses are on the court, and help me decide how I should plan my plays during games.

Scenario Two - The Big Game

One week, Andrew and his team heard from their coach that they were set to play the University of Tennessee that upcoming Saturday. Everyone on the team was pretty worried, as this was their second time playing Tennessee this season, and they had suffered a pretty hard loss during their first game. Andrew was especially nervous, as Tennessee had a really good shooter this year - he’d face some difficulty defending against him.

Before practice on Monday, Andrew went on StatTrackr and pulled up the cumulative stats of Tennessee’s team. On the team’s page, he could see both their overall stats, such as their three point percentage, and some videos from their most recent games. After looking at these stats, Andrew scrolled through the team’s roster to find their star shooter, and looked through his profile. Next to the shooter’s season statistics, his highlight reel showed some clips of his recent wins, which seemed to have been uploaded the night before.

Andrew watched these clips, and figured out that this shooter, while excellent when shooting threes from the front, usually struggled when shooting from the side. Knowing this, Andrew was able to create a thorough game plan for Saturday.


User Stories

  • As an athlete, I need to be able to compare my team’s overall statistics against teams from other schools because that will help me and my teammates understand what skills we as a collective excel at, and where we may need to improve when preparing for a future game.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to compare my own statistics against the stats of players from other teams because that can help me prepare myself specifically against certain players from other teams, who I can expect to compete against more so than other members of the enemy team.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to compare the stats between players on another team because I’ll be more prepared when planning for an upcoming game when I can see who my toughest opponent may be.
  • As an athlete, I want to be easily able to find a certain player through their team roster because it would make seeing a teams performance easier to do, by having the ability to quickly.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to view another player’s profile because it can be useful identifying certain athletes after a game, for communication purposes outside of playing.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to see a player’s highlights, because it can help me gain a better visual understanding of how these statistics relate to their performance.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to see videos of another team’s games, because it can give me a better idea of how my opponents may play as a group.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to upload videos of myself in game, because I want to be able to celebrate my wins within my profile.

Scenario Three - Totally Trending

It had been a great game for Andrew. Towards the end of the last quarter, the score was tied, and every member of the team was anxious. However, at the last minute, just when everyone thought Auburn was going to break the tie, Andrew grabbed a rebound off of an Auburn shot and ran it back to the other side of the court, dunking it in the net just before the buzzer went off. His team, who were lucky enough to film the whole thing, went wild! It was one of the greatest games he had ever played.

That next week, however, was the exact opposite. Within the first half of the game, Andrew not only managed to miss four shots, but also receive three fouls. As a result, he was benched for the rest of the game, which ended in a loss. He felt humiliated that night, and considered skipping practice the next day just so he wouldn’t have to see his teammates.

However, after he got home, he got a notification on StatTrackr. The video of his win, which he had uploaded the day after, had gotten so many likes that he was on the app’s weekly list of featured videos, showcasing it to everyone on StatTrackr. Knowing that so many other athletes thought he looked cool gave Andrew the confidence he needed, and helped him walk into practice the next day feeling ready to play.


User Stories

  • As an athlete, I want to be able to see a player’s highlights, because it can help me gain a better visual understanding of how these statistics relate to their performance.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to upload videos of myself in game, because I want to be able to celebrate my wins within my profile.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to showcase my videos to other users because I want the ability to show off my highlights, and promote myself as a successful player.
  • As an athlete, I want to have a popularity system, or a “like” system in place for videos, because I want to be able to recognize and celebrate both mine and other players’ success.
  • As an athlete, I want to have a leaderboard in place for popular videos, because I want to be able to easily access the best videos on the app.
  • As an athlete, I want to have a rotating leaderboard that only shows popular videos within a set time period, because I want a variety of content within the more entertainment-focused portions of the app.
  • As an athlete, I need to feel like I have a sense of community because a connection among other athletes will increase my confidence, and help me perform better.
  • As an athlete, I want to be able to decide what videos are associated with my account because I want to be able to control my image on my page.
